Test DJIA source

This is a test DJIA source. It has links to the following test DJIA pages.
It is a locally hosted static test version of http://geo.crox.net/djia/yyyy-mm-dd
  • 1980-01-01 - a date in the past, with DJIA
  • 2999-12-31 - a date in the future, with no DJIA yet (returns a 404)
  • 1980-04-03 - Thu 3 April, day 1 of 4 with the same DJIA
  • 1980-04-04 - Fri 4 April, day 2 of 4 with the same DJIA
  • 1980-04-05 - Sat 5 April, day 3 of 4 with the same DJIA
  • 1980-04-06 - Sun 6 April, day 4 of 4 with the same DJIA
  • 1981-01-01 - returns after a two-second delay (requires PHP)
